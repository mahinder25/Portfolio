Youvati Overview

View Process Download App

Redesigned Youvati from Complexity to Clarity,Creating an Intuitive Experience for Beauty Professionals.

Youvati empowers over 13,000 beauty professionals with an all-in-one platform for business management, skill development, community building, and financial support.

To increase user retention and engagement, the platform needed a complete brand and product redesign that not only improves the overall user experience and interface but also supports and scales its wide-ranging feature set.

The core challenge was to transform a complex, feature-rich app into an intuitive and empowering experience that supports beauty entrepreneurs at every stage of their journey.
